# AIpower — AI 平权倡议 / AI Equity Initiative

> 高级 AI 应当属于每一个人，而非被少数巨头与权力机构垄断。
> Advanced AI should belong to everyone — not be monopolized by a few corporations or states.

---

## 中文

**AI 平权倡议（AIpower）** 是一个由志愿者驱动、完全开放的全球性公民组织。我们相信：随着 AI 能力以前所未有的速度跃升，先进 AI 正在迅速成为新时代的"水、电、教育"。如果它最终只属于大公司、富裕国家与特权阶层，普通人将在认知能力、生产效率、机会获取上被结构性甩开。

我们要做的事很简单：

- **倡导**：推动政策、行业与平台朝"普惠 AI"的方向演化。
- **开放**：贡献并保护开源模型、开放数据、开放算力。
- **教育**：让普通人理解、使用并掌控 AI。
- **接入**：通过共享、众筹、低价计划，让买不起前沿模型的人也能用上。
- **监督**：发布报告，揭示 AI 算力、模型、数据的垄断风险。

**口号 / Slogan**：*Advanced AI for everyone. 让先进 AI 属于每一个人。*

## English

**AIpower — AI Equity Initiative** is a volunteer-driven, fully open, global civic organization. We believe that as AI capabilities scale at unprecedented speed, frontier AI is rapidly becoming the *water, electricity, and education* of our era. If it ends up controlled exclusively by large corporations, wealthy nations, and the privileged class, ordinary people will be structurally left behind in cognition, productivity, and opportunity.

Our work is simple:

- **Advocate** — push policy, industry, and platforms toward universal AI access.
- **Open** — contribute to and defend open-source models, open data, open compute.
- **Educate** — help ordinary people understand, use, and control AI.
- **Access** — provide shared, crowdfunded, or low-cost programs so those who cannot afford frontier models can still use them.
- **Watch** — publish reports exposing monopolization risks in AI compute, models, and data.

## 立场声明 / Stance

我们 **不反对** AI 的发展，也 **不反对** 商业公司从 AI 获利。
我们 **反对** 的是：通过算力壁垒、闭源、价格歧视、地域封锁、监管俘获等手段，将"先进 AI 的使用权"变成只有少数人才能享有的特权。

We are **not** against AI development, nor against companies profiting from AI.
We **are** against turning *the right to use advanced AI* into a privilege accessible only to a few — via compute moats, closed weights, price discrimination, geographic blocks, or regulatory capture.
