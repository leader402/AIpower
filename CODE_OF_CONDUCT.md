# 行为准则 / Code of Conduct

## 中文

### 我们的立场

AIpower 是一个跨语言、跨国界、跨行业的公民组织。我们要谈的是 AI 时代最敏感的话题：权力、价格、监管、国族差异、技术鸿沟。议题敏感不是借口，**我们要把"如何讨论敏感议题"做得比任何商业公司都更好。**

### 我们鼓励的行为

- 以理服人；带上数据、来源、案例。
- 区分"我反对你的观点"与"我反对你这个人"。
- 主动给非英语母语者更多耐心。
- 在涉及自身利益（雇主、持仓、地区利益）时主动声明。
- 听到尖锐意见时先尝试问"你能再解释一下吗"，再决定要不要反驳。
- 默认假设对方是出于善意，直到有清晰证据相反。

### 我们禁止的行为

- 人身攻击、辱骂、骚扰、跟踪、人肉。
- 基于种族、国籍、性别、性取向、宗教、残障、年龄的歧视性言论。
- 国族对立式表达（"X 国就是 / X 国人就是"）。
- 把某个 AI 公司或其员工作为攻击目标（我们针对 *结构性问题*，不针对个体）。
- 在仓库内推广商业产品、邀请码、推荐链接。
- 故意散布未经核实的指控；公开他人私人信息。
- 利用本组织名义谋取个人利益。

### 处置流程

1. **轻度违规**：维护者公开评论提醒，记录在案。
2. **中度违规**：禁言 30 天（无法评论、无法投票，可继续浏览）。
3. **严重违规 / 反复违规**：由指导委员会发起表决，2/3 通过则除名。
4. **涉及法律红线**：直接转交相关平台/机构，本组织终止与当事人一切关系。

### 申诉

被处置者可在 30 天内通过新建 Issue 申诉，由全体成员大会投票最终裁定。

### 报告渠道

发现违规行为，请：
- 在相关 Issue/PR 下 @ 任一维护者；或
- 私下联系秘书处邮箱（首届成立后公开）。

我们承诺：**举报人身份保密，除非举报人本人同意公开。**

## English

### Our stance

AIpower is a cross-language, cross-border, cross-industry civic organization. We discuss the most sensitive topics of the AI era: power, price, regulation, national difference, technological divide. Sensitivity is not an excuse — **we will hold ourselves to a higher standard of discourse than any commercial company.**

### What we encourage

- Argue with data, sources, and cases.
- Separate *"I disagree with your view"* from *"I oppose you as a person"*.
- Be extra patient with non-native English speakers.
- Disclose your stakes (employer, equity, regional interest) proactively.
- When you hear a sharp view, ask "can you say more?" before pushing back.
- Assume good faith until clear evidence says otherwise.

### What we prohibit

- Personal attacks, slurs, harassment, stalking, doxxing.
- Discriminatory language based on race, nationality, gender, sexuality, religion, disability, or age.
- Nation-vs-nation generalizations ("Country X is / People from X are…").
- Targeting individual employees of any AI company. We focus on *structural problems*, not individuals.
- Promoting commercial products, referral codes, affiliate links inside this repo.
- Spreading unverified accusations or publishing others' private information.
- Using the AIpower name to advance personal commercial interests.

### Enforcement ladder

1. **Minor** — public reminder by a maintainer; recorded.
2. **Moderate** — 30-day mute (no commenting / voting; can still read).
3. **Severe or repeated** — Council motion; expulsion at two-thirds vote.
4. **Legally over the line** — referred to relevant platforms/authorities; the organization severs all ties.

### Appeal

Anyone subject to enforcement may file an appeal Issue within 30 days. The General Assembly votes on the final outcome.

### Reporting

To report a violation:
- @ any maintainer on the relevant Issue/PR; or
- Email the Secretariat (address will be published once the first Council is seated).

We commit: **the reporter's identity is kept confidential unless they consent to disclosure.**
