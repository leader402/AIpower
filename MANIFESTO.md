# 宣言 / Manifesto

## 中文

### 一、我们身处的时刻

人类历史上第一次，一种通用的"思考能力"被装进了机器，并以指数级速度变得更强。这种能力正在重塑医疗、教育、法律、科研、写作、编程、决策——几乎一切依赖认知的领域。

谁掌握了最强的 AI，谁就在新世纪掌握了：

- 学习与思考的杠杆；
- 经济生产的杠杆；
- 政治表达与社会动员的杠杆。

### 二、我们正在看到的趋势

- **算力垄断**：前沿训练已经只有不到十家公司能负担。
- **闭源化**：最强模型的权重不再公开，普通研究者无法审查、无法复现。
- **价格分层**：先进推理能力被打包进高价订阅，发展中国家的用户被价格门槛阻挡。
- **地域封锁**：因国籍、IP、支付方式，大量人群被排除在先进 AI 之外。
- **监管俘获**：以"安全"为名提出的部分立法，实际抬高了开源与小玩家的门槛。

### 三、我们主张

1. **使用权是基本权利**：在 AI 时代，能否使用先进 AI，关乎机会平等。它应当被视为一种新的数字基本权利，而不是奢侈品。
2. **开源是公共品**：开源模型、开放权重、开放数据集、开放评测，是抗衡垄断的根本基础设施。
3. **普通人优先**：在政策与产品讨论中，普通人、低收入者、被封锁地区的用户的利益必须被优先呈现。
4. **透明高于一切口号**：任何宣称"为了人类福祉"的 AI 实验室，都必须接受社会对其算力、数据、收益、影响的可审计监督。
5. **反对单一权威**：我们反对任何单一国家、单一公司、单一组织垄断"什么是好 AI"的定义权。

### 四、我们承诺

- 我们不接受任何前沿 AI 公司直接控制本组织决策的资金；
- 我们公开本组织全部文档、决策记录、财务往来；
- 我们不发动人身攻击、不搞身份政治、不进行国别对立；
- 我们对所有合作方一视同仁：政府、企业、学界、个人，谁愿意推动 AI 平权，我们就与谁合作。

### 五、我们邀请你

如果你：
- 正在因为价格、地域、语言而用不上最好的 AI；
- 在大公司里工作，但对内部垄断方向感到不安；
- 在做开源、开数据、低成本推理；
- 在做 AI 教育、AI 普及；
- 只是相信"先进 AI 应该属于每一个人"——

**请加入我们。** 详见 [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)。

## English

### I. The moment we are in

For the first time in human history, a general capacity to *think* has been embedded into machines and is improving at exponential speed. This capacity is reshaping medicine, education, law, research, writing, programming, decision-making — almost everything that depends on cognition.

Whoever controls the most capable AI controls, in this new century:

- the leverage of learning and thought;
- the leverage of economic production;
- the leverage of political expression and social mobilization.

### II. What we are seeing

- **Compute monopoly** — frontier training is already affordable to fewer than ten companies on Earth.
- **Closed weights** — the most capable models are no longer publicly inspectable; outside researchers cannot audit or reproduce them.
- **Price stratification** — advanced reasoning is bundled into premium subscriptions; users in developing countries are walled off by price.
- **Geographic blockade** — huge populations are excluded from frontier AI by nationality, IP, or payment infrastructure.
- **Regulatory capture** — some legislation proposed in the name of "safety" effectively raises the barrier for open-source projects and small players.

### III. What we stand for

1. **Access is a basic right.** In the age of AI, whether one can use advanced AI determines equality of opportunity. It must be treated as a new digital basic right, not a luxury good.
2. **Open source is a public good.** Open models, open weights, open datasets, and open evaluations are the foundational infrastructure against monopoly.
3. **Ordinary people first.** In policy and product debates, the interests of ordinary users, low-income users, and users in blocked regions must be represented first.
4. **Transparency above slogans.** Any AI lab that claims to act "for humanity" must accept auditable public oversight of its compute, data, revenue, and impact.
5. **Against any single authority.** We oppose any single nation, company, or organization claiming sole authority over what counts as "good AI."

### IV. Our commitments

- We do **not** accept funding that lets any frontier AI company directly control this organization's decisions.
- We publish all charter documents, decision records, and financial flows.
- We do not engage in personal attacks, identity politics, or nation-vs-nation framing.
- We treat all collaborators equally — governments, companies, academia, individuals: whoever advances AI equity, we work with them.

### V. We invite you

If you are:
- locked out of the best AI by price, geography, or language;
- working inside a major AI lab but uncomfortable with the direction of monopoly;
- building open-source models, open data, low-cost inference;
- doing AI education, AI literacy, AI access programs;
- simply someone who believes *advanced AI should belong to everyone* —

**Join us.** See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).
